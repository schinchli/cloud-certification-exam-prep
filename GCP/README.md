## Study Notes

### [Cloud Digital Leader](./Cloud-Digital-Leader/)
Foundational certification covering cloud concepts, Google Cloud products, and digital transformation.
Topics: Cloud fundamentals, compute, storage, networking, data/ML products, security, pricing

### [Google Cloud Generative AI Leader](./GenAI-Leader/)
Covers generative AI concepts, Google's AI product ecosystem, and enterprise AI strategy.
Topics: Foundation models, Vertex AI, Gemini, RAG, responsible AI, AI use cases
